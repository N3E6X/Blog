# MD Writer

A minimal markdown editor with all features.

## Text Formatting

This is **bold text** and this is *italic text*.

You can also use ~~strikethrough~~ for deleted text.

| Cell 4 | Cell 5 | Cell 6 |
| Cell 4 | Cell 5 | Cell 6 |
| Cell 4 | Cell 5 | Cell 6 |
